The Great Lake State

Can you name the 5 Great Lakes? Here they are ranked by surface area largest to smallest.

  1. Lake Superior: It get's it's name for a reason.
  2. Lake Huron: It's connected to Lake Michigan is that cheating?
  3. Lake Michigan: The only Great Lake that ONLY touches U.S soil.
  4. Lake Erie: Erie get's some of it's water from the Detroit river.
  5. Lake Ontario: Smallest by surface area but it is deeper than it's neighbor Lake Erie.

Great facts about Great Lakes

The Great Lakes are known as a binational region as it stretches over 2 Canadian territories and 8 great U.S. States. They are the largest group of freshwater lakes on earth by area and second by total volume.
